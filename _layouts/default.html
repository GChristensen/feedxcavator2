Description

Have you dreamt of something that crawls your beloved forums and brings the new threads to your news aggregator? That allows to subscribe to your favorite social network post updates through RSS? That can deliver headlines from the received mail in the form of a feed? Here it is. Feedxcavator is capable to accomplish anything of the described above and can do even more.

This Google App Engine application is able to convert anything to RSS with an arbitrary level of fine-tuning. It has a built-in WebSub server for real-time feed updates and allows to program a feed-extraction process in a simple Clojure-based DSL directly through the web-UI (see a demo). Find more at the project GitHub page.
